Section C: Case Study (30 marks)

Case Study: EcoWear Startup Mia, a Grade 10 student, starts EcoWear, a small business selling eco-friendly T-shirts made from recycled materials. She operates as a sole trader from her home in Sydney, selling online and at local markets. Mia’s goals are to grow her business and promote sustainability. She faces rising costs due to supply chain issues and competition from larger clothing brands. Mia is considering a loan to buy equipment and partnering with a friend to share workload and costs.

18. Identify the sector EcoWear operates in and explain why. (4 marks)

19. Conduct a SWOT analysis for EcoWear (one point per category: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats). (8 marks)

20. Recommend whether Mia should take a loan or form a partnership. Justify with two reasons. (6 marks)

21. Using STEEPLE, identify one Economic factor affecting EcoWear and suggest how Mia could respond. (6 marks)

22. Outline two financial elements Mia should track to ensure EcoWear’s success. (6 marks)

Section D: Essay Question (20 marks)
